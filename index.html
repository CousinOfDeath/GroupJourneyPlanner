Group Journey Planner

This mobile app makes planning a group journey easier whether you are teacher, HR manager or hobby group leader, planning a field trip.

Try demo

Search route

Search and save optimal routes for groups using public transport. The application searches an optimal route for your group and divides it into several routes if necessary to help the whole group to reach the destination on time.

Share your journey

Share the planned journey with the other group members. In case of changes in the journey or possible disruption, you can share the updated plan with others.

Get disruption alerts

When you have saved your journey you will receive alerts if there is a disruption on your route. An alternative route will be proposed for you or you can easily re-plan your journey.
